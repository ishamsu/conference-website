About College


Malabar Institute of Technology, ISO 9001-2008 certified, an integrated campus situated in the 250 acres of lush green cinnamon belt at Anjarakandy, houses premium educational institutions, Super Specialty Hospital, Kannur Medical College, Dental College, College of Nursing, College of Pharmacy, Institute of Paramedical Sciences and Institute of Engineering & Technology. The College Management has chosen this backward area for establishing the college with the Aim of sharing the responsibility of uplifting the people of the area by making Technical Education affordable and within reach. The Management aims at creating a Centre of excellence in Engineering Technology.

About Conference

RTET'20 invites distinguished researchers, scientists, students, teachers and eminent authors in academic and industry working for the advancements in Science, Engineering and technology to explore with us, together, various upcoming innovations in such areas, challenges often faced in them and to confer to find solutions for them. We aim to bring together Academicians, Scientists, Research Scholars and Students, online to share and disseminate information about scientific research works and practical challenges encountered and the solutions adopted, in view of COVID-19 Pandemic. We believe that the conference will create a path to establish a research relation for the authors and listeners with opportunities for National collaboration and networking among the universities and Institutions from India for promoting research and developing technologies.
